# AnkaLife — Lead Generation Playbook
### How to build an endless stream of consumers filling out the survey

Your marketplace only works if leads keep flowing in. This is the complete plan to make that
happen — organized by cost, with the economics that make it self-sustaining ("endless").

---

## 0. The Flywheel (the whole game in one picture)
```
   Run cheap/organic + paid traffic  →  people fill out the SURVEY (consented leads)
                ↑                                        ↓
   more agents join (deeper inventory)        scored leads land in the marketplace
                ↑                                        ↓
        margin funds MORE traffic   ←   agents BUY leads (you earn the spread)
```
"Endless" isn't one magic channel — it's a **self-funding loop**: every dollar of lead
margin (and every agent's own referral traffic) buys more leads, which attracts more agents,
which buys more leads. Your job is to keep **blended cost-per-lead (CPL) below your sell price**
and keep the loop spinning.

### The one economic rule
You sell exclusive leads for **$12–$33**. So your **blended CPL must stay under that** to profit.
- Free/organic + agent-referral traffic = ~$0 CPL → pure margin.
- Paid (Meta/Google) = ~$10–$30 CPL → thin-to-good margin; optimize down.
- **Blend them.** Most winning lead businesses run paid near break-even to grab market share,
  and profit on organic + referrals + aged-lead resale + agent retention.

---

## 1. FREE / owned channels — start here ($0–low CPL)
1. **Agent referral links (already built).** Every agent gets a personal survey link + QR. When
   they share it, the lead is theirs, exclusive, and costs *you* nothing. Push agents to share it
   everywhere — this is free inventory that also makes agents stickier. *Biggest near-term lever.*
2. **Short-form video (TikTok / Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts).** Educational/relatable clips
   ("how much does final expense really cost?", "what happens to your family's bills if…").
   Link in bio → survey. Free, compounding, and final-expense/senior content performs huge here.
3. **SEO content + a free tool.** Blog posts ("how much life insurance do I need," "best burial
   insurance for seniors") + a **coverage calculator / quiz lead magnet** that ends in the survey.
   Ranks on Google over time → free, high-intent fills. *(I can build the calculator + content pages.)*
4. **Existing-client & friends/family referrals.** "Refer someone, you both…" Simple and warm.
5. **Organic social posts, groups, Q&A** (Facebook groups, Nextdoor, Reddit/Quora answers — helpful,
   not spammy) with the survey link.

## 2. PAID acquisition — scalable ($10–$30 CPL)
6. **Meta (Facebook/Instagram) lead ads — the #1 channel for life/final-expense.** Target by age,
   life events (new baby, new home, recently married), homeowners, interests. Final-expense FB leads
   run **~$10–$30 each** ([Aged Lead Store](https://agedleadstore.com/final-expense-facebook-leads-worth-it/)).
   Start ~$175/week → ~10 leads/week, then scale what converts.
7. **Google Search ads — highest intent.** Bid on "life insurance quote," "final expense / burial
   insurance," "mortgage protection." Pricier per click but people are actively shopping.
8. **TikTok ads** — cheap reach; surprisingly strong for final expense + younger term buyers.
9. **YouTube ads** (in-stream + Shorts) — explainer creative → survey.
10. **Bing/Microsoft ads** — cheaper than Google, skews older (great for FE/senior/Medicare).
11. **Nextdoor ads** — local, older homeowners.
12. **Native ads (Taboola/Outbrain)** — "quiz/calculator" style content that funnels to the survey.
13. **Retargeting pixels** (Meta/Google) — re-capture people who started the survey but didn't finish.

## 3. OFFLINE / direct response — the final-expense powerhouse
14. **Direct-mail lead cards** — THE classic FE channel. Mail to "turning 65 / new Medicare,"
    "new homeowner," or senior-homeowner lists; recipients mail back or call/scan a QR → that's an
    *inbound, consented* lead. (Mail isn't TCPA-restricted; their response = consent.)
15. **Local events** — senior expos, health fairs, church bulletins, community boards (use your QR code).
16. **Radio / podcast ads** — Christian, news/talk, and senior-targeted radio convert well for FE.
17. **Flyers / door hangers with the QR code** in target neighborhoods.

## 4. PARTNERSHIPS (B2B2C) — warm, cheap, high-trust
18. **Funeral homes & cremation services** → final expense (perfect alignment).
19. **Realtors & mortgage brokers** → mortgage protection (new homeowners).
20. **Senior centers, retirement communities, churches, veterans' groups** → FE/veteran leads.
21. **Financial advisors, CPAs/tax preparers, estate attorneys** → cross-referrals.
22. **Employers / HR / gig platforms** → voluntary/term coverage.
Give partners a co-branded survey link or QR; optionally pay a small referral fee per submission.

## 5. SEED inventory while you ramp
23. **Buy wholesale/aged leads** to stock the marketplace on day one (so agents see depth), while
    your owned channels spin up. Resell them as your "Aged" tier.
24. **Co-registration networks** — consumers opting into multiple offers at once (compliance-vetted only).

## What I can build on the site to maximize conversion
- **Vertical landing pages** (Final Expense, Veteran, Mortgage Protection, Term) so each ad matches a
  tailored page → the survey. Matching ad→page→survey can double conversion.
- **Free calculator / quiz lead magnet** ("How much coverage do I need?") that ends in the survey.
- **Retargeting pixels** + **abandoned-survey SMS/email** follow-up.
- **Open Graph / link-preview tags** so the shared survey link looks great on social.
- (Built already: the survey link, **QR code**, and **agent referral funnels**.)

## 30 / 60 / 90-day ramp
- **Days 1–30:** turn on agent referral links (free), launch 1–2 TikTok/Reels accounts, stand up the
  calculator + 2–3 landing pages, seed inventory with a small wholesale batch. Start a small Meta test (~$175/wk).
- **Days 31–60:** scale the Meta campaigns that convert, add Google Search for high-intent, sign 2–3
  partnerships (a funeral home + a realtor), start a direct-mail test for FE.
- **Days 61–90:** double down on the best 2–3 channels by CPL, add retargeting + abandoned-survey
  follow-up, recruit agents harder (their referral traffic compounds your inventory for free).

## Compliance (non-negotiable)
Every lead must carry **TCPA prior express written consent** (your survey already captures it) and be
**DNC-scrubbed before calling**. Consent is exactly what makes a submission a *sellable, callable* lead —
no consent, no product. Disclose, honor opt-outs, keep records.

---

**Bottom line:** start with the free levers (agent referrals + organic video + the calculator), seed a
little inventory, then pour paid + partnerships on top — and keep blended CPL under your sell price so
the flywheel funds itself.
